Information about the band:

Formed in:

2006, Kirkland, WA, United States.

Members:

Robin Pecknold (vocals, guitar), Skyler Skjelset (guitar, mandolin, vocals), Casey Wescott (keyboards, mandolin, vocals), Bryn Lumsden (bass, 2006), Craig Curran (bass, 2006-08), Nick Peterson (drums, percussion, vocals, 2006-08), Christian Wargo (bass, guitar, vocals, 2008-present), J. Tillman (drums, percussion, vocals, 2008-12), Morgan Henderson (double bass, guitar, woodwinds, violin, percussion, saxophone, 2010-present), Matt Barrick (drums, percussion, 2017-present).

Genres:

Indie Folk, Chamber Folk, Progressive Folk, Chamber Pop, Indie Pop, Indie Rock.

Most relevant projects:

  • Sun Giant - The band's second EP, released on April 8th, 2008. Even though the group considered the release not representative of their ambition, it turned out receiving a wide critical acclaim.
  • Fleet Foxes - Following their impactful EP, on June 3rd, 2008, the self-titled debut studio album is released, getting even more praise by both fans and critics to the point that some might consider this record one of the best debuts of all time.
  • Helplessness Blues - No need to say that the group's following LP, released on May 3rd, 2011 also received worldly acclaim, going on to be nominated for Best Folk Album for the 54th Grammy Awards and peaking at number 4 on the Billboard 200, the band's highest position on the chart to date.
  • Crack-up - Their most ambitious project, released on June 16th, 2017. Despite not having an equal praise from the fans when compared to previous works, it contains the most experimental and forward-thinking songs among all their discography.
